Stars Afrikan Dream LTD

Stars Afrikan Dream Limited is a registered company which has established Afrikan Dream School of Fashion and Fashion Centre in Manso Agroyesum in the Amansie South District. The School of Fashion and Fashion Centre was launched on 29 November, 2020. The school has hired a fashion designer who trains interested people in the community in fashion and has accepted thirteens (13) students from the town who are currently training to become fashion designers without any tuition or admission fee. At the end of every course, which lasts for two (2) years, three (3) needy but passionate people from the community will be accepted into the school without having to pay any tuition or admission fees. The Afrikan Dream School of Fashion and Fashion Centre will train and equip the youth of Manso Agroyesum with skills to work with for their personal development and the development of the community.
